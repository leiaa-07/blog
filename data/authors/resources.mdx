there are SOO many great free resources out there, so here are just my top picks :D
### for math contests:
- **youtube**: [Art of Problem Solving](https://www.youtube.com/@ArtofProblemSolving/videos)\, [Daily Challenge with Po-Shen Loh](https://www.youtube.com/@PoShenLoh)\, [Terry Chew Academy](https://www.youtube.com/@TerryChewAcademy)\, [Sohil Rathi](https://www.youtube.com/@SohilRathi)\, [Chan Lye Lee](https://www.youtube.com/@chanlyelee)\, [Chen Hongming](https://www.youtube.com/@ChenHongming/videos), [Math Divulged](https://www.youtube.com/@mathdivulged1092/videos)
- **websites**: [AoPS Alcumus](https://artofproblemsolving.com/alcumus) (i grinded this sm during quarantine era lol)\, [MathDash](https://mathdash.live/) (essentially the cf of math)\, [AMC Course](https://math.llmlab.io/courses) (to train past AMC problems by topic)
- **books**: [Omega Learn Books](https://www.omegalearn.org/books)\, [Math Competitions I](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1B-gJJQUtcOKAaHeNGzRL1assWxEwtu0j/view)\, [Math Competitions II](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1qCZh7ro6UfbmRivpBafEmJ0HRXNh0H3t/view)
